CHAI & VIBES

COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

  • Inclusive Though we are a Muslim-friendly space (providing prayer areas, serving halal food and drinks) we are not a Muslim-exclusive space. Chai & Vibes is and always will be a space welcome to all races, religions, genders, sexual orientations, and ages.
  • Community-Building The purpose of our space is to build community and create long-lasting friendships. Though you may find networking opportunities or your soulmate along the way, our sole purpose is to ensure you feel comfortable, welcomed, and encouraged to create connections with friends and strangers.
  • Cultivating Collaboration Chai & Vibes strives to create a platform that can serve to uplift performers, artists, and vendors. We try to ensure that these individuals align with the same mission & values. We expect them to respect our space and attendees, and ask that our attendees give them the love, respect, and graciousness they deserve. If you are interested in collaborating with us please contact us via instagram on @chaiandvibes

Attendee Promise:

In order to maintain a safe, inclusive, and comfortable space, as an attendee of our events we expect you to promise the following:

    ​
  • I will not engage in or tolerate physically or verbally harmful language or actions.
    • ​
  • I will not engage in or tolerate racism, sexism, ageism, homophobia/transphobia, or hate speech directed at specific religious, sectarian, or cultural communities.
  • ​I will not engage in or tolerate predatory behavior. If I feel that someone is abusing their role or power, or making others uncomfortable, I will speak out to the Chai & Vibes organizers.
  • ​I will not engage in or tolerate any form of sexual harassment or misconduct targeted towards any sex or gender.
    • ​
  • I will be mindful of other people's space, boundaries, and comfort when approaching others and engaging in conversation
    • ​
  • I will be respectful of the space we're in, being mindful of those that are there who may not be attendees, and respecting the maintenance of property and cleanliness.
    • ​
  • I will be honest in paying for goods and services (chai, food, vendors) as requested. For the chai & vibes team, all the proceeds from our chai and baked goods go directly back to funding future events.
  • ​I will not take photos or videos of others or post them without their explicit consent

Notes:

  • ​If you feel that any of these guidelines have been violated by others, or ANYTHING has occurred to make you feel uncomfortable or unsafe, please reach out to one of our organizers (Ahmad, Haroon, Maisha, Tumi, Aaliya, Izma) so we can support you in handling the situation immediately.
  • You can also text us at (C&V number) or contact us via social media during the event and one of us will come find you whilst maintaining discretion of your concern. ​
  • Chai & Vibes reserves the right to refuse service to anyone.
  • ​Non-compliance to the above guidelines will result in a 2-strike policy: we will discuss the circumstance of your actions, but after two warnings you will no longer be welcome at our events.
  • By signing up, you are agreeing to maintain all the above guidelines and ensuring that an guests that accompany you do the same.
